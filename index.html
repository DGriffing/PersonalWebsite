About

Hi, I’m Diana.

I am a full stack UX designer, from Montana, passionate about education technology, social justice, and cats. I have called Seattle home for five years, taking inspiration from the culture of resilience, creativity, and collaboration that surrounds me. When I am not designing, you can find me rock climbing, designing and DMing my Dungeons & Dragons campaign, or volunteer mentoring in my community’s preschool for homeless youth, First Place.

I will soon graduate from the Informatics department at the University of Washington, and will be available for hire Summer / Fall 2017. Reach out to me if you would like to work together.
